Common Issues

Login Issues
Make sure your Google account is active and properly connected.

Upload / Download Issues
Check your internet connection and storage permissions.

App Crashing
Try restarting or reinstalling the app.

Sync Problems
Ensure your internet connection is stable.

Invite / Share Not Working
Check if your friends have accepted the invite and are using the latest app version.

Friends Not Showing / Adding Issues
Make sure both users are logged in and using the correct account.

FAQs

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account directly from the app or visit our Account Deletion page.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data is securely stored using encrypted systems. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
Can multiple people upload photos in one folder?
Yes, iPic allows real-time uploads from multiple users into shared folders.
Why are my photos not syncing?
This usually happens due to poor internet connectivity. Try switching networks or restarting the app.
