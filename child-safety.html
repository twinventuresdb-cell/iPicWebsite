iPic Child Safety & CSAE Standards Policy

Zero Tolerance Policy

iPic is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have a strict, zero-tolerance policy against the sharing, distribution, or promotion of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

User Reporting

Users can report any concerning content directly within the iPic app by tapping the "More Options" icon on any shared media and selecting "Report Media," followed by the "Child Safety / CSAM" category.

Enforcement & Compliance

Any accounts found violating this policy will be immediately and permanently terminated.

iPic complies with all relevant child safety laws. We report any instances of CSAM/CSAE to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline, and to relevant local and national law enforcement authorities.
